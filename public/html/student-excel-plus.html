Protection Features
  • Accidental Death

    Pays the insured student's beneficiary the principal amount covered for death occurring within one year from the date of accident and resulting from injuries sustained

  • Permanent Total Disablement

    Pays you the principal amount covered:

    • For the loss of two or more limbs or both eyes.
    • For permanent total disablement from gainful employment of any kind, other than dismemberment, provided such disability has continued for a period of 52 weeks and is total, continuous or permanent at the end of this period.
  • Permanent Partial Disablement

    pays you a percentage of the principal amount covered for permanent loss of use of body parts, according to the following schedule:

  • Double Death Benefit

    In the event of a fatal accident within school premises.

  • Accidental Medical Expense

    Reimburses the actual cost, up to the amount selected for medical or surgical treatment, including trained nurses and hospitalization, incurred within 52 weeks from the date of accident and resulting from injuries sustained.

  • Burial Benefit

    In case of fatal accident or murder and assault pays for all actual funeral expenses incurred up to Php 5,000.

  • Cash Assistance

    Your protection for any bodily injury whilst riding a Motorcycle or any two-wheeled vehicle.

  • Cash Allowance

    Cash allowance per day of confinement pays the selected amount, up to maximum of 25 days, to defray for incidental expenses while the insured person was hospitalized due to accident.

  • Third Party Liability

    The school shall be covered for property damage to third party up to Php 50,000. Deductible for Third Party Damage - 5% each and every loss; minimum of Php 5,000.
