COVID-19 Coverage

Cocogen Travel Excel Plus now offers COVID-19 coverage for international trips:

Medical Expense and Hospitalization

Transport and Repatriation in case of Illness.

All benefits apply if caused by COVID-19. Coverage is effective for 15 days only from the date of departure.

  • Conditions

    • Age Limit Based on selected Travel Excel Plan
    • Applicant must present negative test result of valid RT-PCR COVID-19, administered within seventy-two (72) hours before initial actual departure from the Philippines.
    • COVID-19 RT-PCR test must have been administered by any of the Department of Health (DOH) authorized and licensed laboratory, clinics, hospitals, and medical facilities.
    • Applicant should not be a confirmed positive (asymptomatic or symptomatic), suspected, or probably case of COVID-19 at the time of the application.
